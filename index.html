Our Mission

Nurturing Nature, One Place at a Time

At Fathima Balika Muslim Maha Vidyalaya, our Environmental Project brings together students, teachers, and Old Girls' Association members to transform spaces across our school into living classrooms. From gardens and compost stations to rainwater harvesting and wildlife habitats, each location tells a story of care, commitment, and community action for a sustainable future.
